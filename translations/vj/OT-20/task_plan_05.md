## Key Terms for This Chapter
- strange woman - ngoai phu (adulteress/foreign woman)
- honeycomb - mat ong (honey)
- wormwood - dang cay (bitter herb)
- hell - hoa nguc / dia nguc
- cistern - gieng nuoc
- hind - con huou (deer/doe)
- roe - con hoang (roe deer)
- ravished - say dam (intoxicated/enchanted)

## Decisions Made
| Decision | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| strange woman -> nguoi phu nu la/doi | Following context of Proverbs about adultery |
| hell -> dia nguc | Standard Protestant term for sheol/hell context |
